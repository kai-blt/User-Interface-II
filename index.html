Full Stack Developer - Musician

Trombonist and music educator Brendan Lai-Tong, was born in Johannesburg, South Africa and is a lifelong appreciator of all things that create sound. He enjoys making music with a myriad of Bay Area ensembles spanning multiple genres including - classical, contemporary avant-garde, rock, jazz & reggae. He is a member of the San Francisco Contemporary Music Players

Additionally, Brendan has experience in the field of Email Marketing and Graphics Design and is comfortable working with HTML/CSS, Adobe Photoshop, GIMP, and various ESP platforms (Bronto, Cheetahmail, Mailchimp). He is working to become a Full Stack Developer and started attending the Lambda School in August 2020.
